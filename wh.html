Warhammer!

Warhammer and Warhammer 40K (perhaps some 30K too) miniatures both painting and playing. People willing to help in any aspect.

So you want to fight heresy..

Well it's expensive. Warhammer minatures isn't a hobby you want to have second thoughts about. Like all hobbies, painting and winning with your personal armies is a rewarding experience but it requires commitment. But you'll never have more fun that imagining a giant Space Marine fight a Demon in close combat on the top of an abandonded tower.
