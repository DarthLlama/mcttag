Dungeons and Dragons!

Saturdays are reserved to let out the inner Mage (or Warrior or Rogue or Bard) the Rise of the Endless Game is currently on, however, new players are always welcome.

So you want to roleplay..

It's easy. All it will take is time and effort and to have someone with an interest in making a story and a world for their players to explore and ruin within a few hours. The images to the left are all related to the McTag game; The Rise of the Endless. The Hydra symbol taking the place of a giant Leviathan that threatens to destroy the world of Qadron.

Here you can find characters sheets, pre-made scenarios (for players to ruin) as well as loads of other related information that an aspiring roleplayer can take a look at.
