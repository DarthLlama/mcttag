Board Games!

General board game games are always running when members get sick of whater they're doing. the Magic Tournement had a lot of fun playing Code Words and Cash and Guns throughout the day.

So you want to.. board a game?

There's no dedicated day for board games, it's pretty much a free-for-fall however, there is always one person who suggests a game of Pendulum and what plans we had- poof. Gone. So if you enjoy being with a few friends playing some quirky game for (generally) 20 to 60 minutes then this will be the section for you.


Ca$h 'n Guns

At MCM Comicon we had a blast playing some board games under the CEX Inflatable Shark. This was the one that we played before we got kicked out at the end.

Once Upon a Time

To sharpen the razor wit and story telling of the DnD games, the Dungeon Master's Guide reccomends this game. So do we. Hilarious, fun, and you get a cool (read: completly moronic) fairytale at the end.

Codewords

Ever wanted to be a spy? Like word association games? Then Codewords is for you. This is the superspy-esque game for family nad friends. Perfect if you have an even number of players.
