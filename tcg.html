Friday Night Magic!

Trading Cards a plenty, any type, every Friday. Magic the Gathering is currently our focus but there is a pretty strong Yugioh group too.

So you want to draw cards..

It's easy. Like with some other hobbies, there may be some cost. However, trading cards are some high quality products and the games themselves are reasonably easy to learn. The youngest member of McTag, Kamran (9) has recently learnt how to play Yugioh. His win rate is still low (he's 9) but focus on the fact that he can play.


How to Play Yugioh

A pretty good site to learn... well, how to play Yugioh. By itself, it's pretty dry, but if you've got a few workable decks, even a 9 year old can learn.
